Why Juvion

We are building something that matters. Here's what you can expect working with us.

Science First

Work at the intersection of biology and AI, building platforms grounded in rigorous experimental science, not hype.

Real Impact

Accelerate discovery of interventions for aging and neurodegenerative diseases. Your work contributes directly to treatments that improve human lives.

Diverse and Innovative Team

Collaborate with world-class scientists, entrepreneurs, and advisors in a flat, open, and intellectually honest culture.

Flexible & Remote-friendly

Hybrid setup based in Lausanne with flexibility for remote work where the role allows.

Meaningful Work

Every project touches real disease biology and contributes to improving patient outcomes.

Competitive Compensation

Salaries benchmarked to Swiss biotech standards, reviewed annually.

Learning Budget

Annual budget for conferences, courses, and scientific literature subscriptions.

Flat, Open Culture
