Our Approach

Science-Backed Solutions
for Healthy Aging

Neuroscience

Motor terminal deterioration during aging disrupts muscle control. We target the neuromuscular junction to restore the signaling pathways that keep people moving.

Technology

Proprietary screening platform combining robotics, machine vision, and AI to quantify motor function with unprecedented throughput and precision.

Therapeutics

Plant-derived compounds with reduced regulatory complexity. Juvion-001 increases motor ability during middle and old age, validated across our platform.

FlyGym Platform

Powered by
Drosophila Research

FlyGym is our proprietary robotics and AI platform for high-throughput screening of compounds that affect motor function — using Drosophila melanogaster as a validated model for human aging.

The fruit fly shares 75% of human disease genes, enabling rapid, cost-effective discovery at a scale impossible with vertebrate models.
